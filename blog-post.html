Matcha Muffin

Ingredients

Flour - 488g

Baking Soda - 2g

Baking Powder - 8g

Matcha Powder - 60g

Sugar - 288g

Butter - 288g

Salt - 4g

Egg - 288g

Water - 190g

Chocolate Chip - 160g

Steps

1) Add butter and sugar to a bowl and mix them. After, slowly pour the egg into the bowl while mixing them.

2) Add flour, matcha powder, chocolate chip and water.
